# Snake & Ladder 20s Promo Design

## Style Prompt
Create a bright mobile-game promo that feels playful, fast, and family-friendly while staying close to the actual Android app. Use the app's soft pastel surfaces and royal-blue action color, with cartoon board footage as the main motion layer. The visual language should suggest a modern version of a classic board game: clear type, large readable claims, rounded mobile UI forms, quick dice-driven pacing, and a confident closing card.

## Colors
- Background wash: `#F3F8FF`
- Primary action blue: `#0D47A1`
- Soft lavender panel: `#EDE6F5`
- Warm board cream: `#FFF4D6`
- Snake red accent: `#E83A33`
- Text: `#1B1B1F`

## Typography
- Headlines: `Avenir Next`, `Arial Rounded MT Bold`, system sans-serif fallback.
- Supporting text: `Avenir Next`, `Inter`, system sans-serif fallback.

## Motion
- Use short 0.35s to 0.7s entrances with y/scale/opacity transforms.
- Keep footage visible as the hero; overlays should support, not cover, board action.
- Use finite ambient motion only. No infinite repeats.
- Pace scenes as five clear beats across exactly 20 seconds.

## What NOT to Do
- Do not switch to neon, dark sci-fi, or casino styling.
- Do not bury actual game footage behind abstract shapes.
- Do not use long explanatory copy or dense feature grids.
- Do not introduce unrelated brand colors beyond the app palette.
